How it works

Lean, but checked.

01 — Draft

A fast local model drafts.

Most coding is routine. A small, cheap model handles it in-house — no frontier bill, no round-trip.

02 — Verify

Every answer hits the wall.

The draft is run, tested, and checked against what correctness requires. We don't trust the output — we verify it.

03 — Escalate

Only the hard part escalates.

When verification can't vouch for it, and only then, Tīrtha calls a frontier model to finish the job.
